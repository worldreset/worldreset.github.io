Niagara Falls International Open

May 22, 2025

Hello everyone! This is my first blog post uwu I had the opportunity to participate in the Niagara Falls International Open this past weekend. It is a scrabble tournament that runs every year, with 22 games over 3 days. I travelled with a friend and we stayed in a cheap motel for the duration the tournament took place. We also did not realise food would be so expensive, so we ended up eating subway and tim hortons (the cheapest places in the area).
